About WeatherCast

Your ultimate destination for accurate and beautiful weather forecasting

What is WeatherCast?

WeatherCast is a modern, beautiful weather forecasting application built with Django and powered by the AccuWeather API. Get real-time weather conditions, hourly forecasts, and 5-day predictions for any city in the world.

Featuring a stunning glassmorphism UI design, smooth animations, and dynamic weather effects, WeatherCast makes checking the weather an enjoyable experience.
